Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At KuroWabi, we value transparency and are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect information directly from you when you interact with our website, specifically when you sign up for our newsletter or submit an inquiry through our atelier contact form.

  • Identity Data: Full name and email address.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, location, and page engagement statistics collected via cookies.
  • Communications: Details of inquiries sent via forms or email support.

2. How We Use Your Data

We utilize the collected information to personalize your KuroWabi experience, deliver requested editorial newsletters, and respond to design project inquiries. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Deliver design inspiration, guides, and lookbook newsletters.
  • Respond to general questions, press inquiries, and design consultations.
  • Analyze web traffic metrics to refine our site's user experience.

3. Third-Party Integrations & Cookies

Our website utilizes cookies to evaluate audience metrics. We also share anonymous data with analytics tools to understand visitor interactions. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third-party advertisers.

Some of our articles may contain affiliate links. These platforms use cookies to track referral traffic. These cookies do not store any personal identity parameters and are strictly used to attribute affiliate conversions.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal details from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes detailed in this policy.

5. Your Choices & Rights

You have full control over your data. You may opt out of our editorial newsletter at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in the footer of our emails. You can also request to inspect, correct, or permanently delete your stored contact details by emailing studio@kurowabi.com.
