Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. We collect personal data when you voluntarily sign up for our newsletter or submit a contact inquiry on this website. This includes:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (when submitted via forms)
  • Message details or collaboration concepts

2. How We Use Your Data

Your details are processed to send you the requested PDF guides, studio updates, newsletters, or to answer your specific service inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party marketers.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use essential cookies to maintain page layouts and minimal, privacy-centric analytics to trace site performance. None of this data is used to personally identify you or build advertising profiles.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to request that your personal information be removed from our database, please contact us at studio@kurowabi.com.
